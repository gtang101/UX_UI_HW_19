About Me

Hi, my name is Gary.
Thank you for wanting to know more about me :D

In 2020, I enrolled in a UX/UI boot camp to learn more about design, in hope of one day, turning my ideas into real products. Unexpectedly, I fall in love with UX design. Since then, I have tried to grow myself into a better UX researcher.

My love for research stems from my academic background in psychology and sociology. Where possible, I try to apply my knowledge in human perception, cognition and behaviour to create intuitive and compelling user experience.

When I am not working or studying (or :sleeping:), I love to enjoy a good narrative (in any format). I am also big on food and want to try out all the cuisine of the world!
